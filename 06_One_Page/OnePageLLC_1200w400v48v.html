Design of a 1200 W LLC Resonant DC-DC Converter (400 V to 48 V)

Electrical Specifications
Input Voltage 360V – 400V (nom. 380V)
Output Voltage 42V – 54V (nom. 48V)
Output Power 1200W
Switching Freq. 95 kHz to 150 kHz
Output Ripple < 0.25V pk-pk
Insulation Resistance > 100MΩ
HV Side Discharge Below 60V @ 5s
Efficiency Up to 91% at full load
Ambient Temperature -20/60°C
Hardware and Software Tools
Schematic and Layout kiCad (PCB-Maker : JLCPCB)
Simulation LTspice / Ngspice / PySpice
Calculation and reporting Python / Jupyterlab / VsCode
Firmware Development STM32CubeIDE and Arduino IDE
Measurement Equipment TDS3034b, TCP202, Yokogawa 700924
Schematic / Topology
Primary Topology Half Bridge
Secondary Topology Synchronous rectifier with center tap
Magnetic cores TDK Planar ELP
Communication Isolated SPI 9Mbps
Power supplies FLyBack 400V/12V, buck 12V/3.3V, buck 48V/12V, LDO 12v/3.3v
Assembled DC-DC converter
Dimensions 300 mm x 140 mm x 47 mm
Weight 1.2 kg
Cooling Forced Air Cooled Heatsinks
Winding assemblyPlanar Transformer and Inductor
MOSFET thermal interfaceHorizontal Tab-Up, Thermal pad 0.3 mm
Key Components
Controller IC STM32F103C8T6 (MINI)
Primary MOSFET (TO-247-3) IPW60R037P7 600V 76A 37mΩ
Second MOSFET (TO-247-3) IRFP4568PBF 150 V 171A 4.8mΩ
Primary Driver UCC21520Q (Source 4A Sink 6A)
Transformer core ELP102/20/38 with I102/7/38 N97
Resonant Inductor core ELP43/10/28 N87
PCB FR4, 4 layers, 1.6 mm, 35µm/70µm
FlyBack controller and core TNY264, ELP 22/6/16 with I 22/2.5/16
