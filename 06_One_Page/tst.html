Design Highlights
  • Synchronous rectification → low conduction loss
  • Gate driver bootstrap — no aux supply needed
  • OCP / OVP / thermal shutdown built-in
  • Soft-start: 2ms ramp, no inrush overshoot
  • 4-layer PCB — power plane + solid GND plane
